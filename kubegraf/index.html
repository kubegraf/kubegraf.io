AI-native Kubernetes control plane

What is KubeGraf?

KubeGraf is a local-first, AI-native Kubernetes control plane that helps DevOps, SRE, and platform engineers understand and operate clusters faster, whether they prefer a terminal, a web dashboard, or a modern single-page application.

Product overview

One control plane, three experiences

KubeGraf is designed to feel native in the way you already work. It brings together a fast terminal UI, a focused web dashboard, and a rich SPA so that you can explore resources, inspect events, and execute changes from a single mental model.

The Brain Panel layers AI-native analysis on top of your Kubernetes events and telemetry, suggesting next actions and surfacing risky changes before they ship.

Local-first by default Terminal, web, & SPA AI-assisted troubleshooting

Brand ownership & naming

The term KubeGraf on this site refers exclusively to the product distributed via kubegraf.io and related installers and binaries.
